HER Models

Simplified (Volmer–Heyrovsky) and Full (Volmer–Heyrovsky–Tafel).

Volmer: $$\ce{H2O + e- + M <-->[{k1}][{k1r}] MH + OH-}$$
Heyrovsky: $$\ce{H2O + MH + e- <-->[{k2}][{k2r}] H2 + OH-}$$
Tafel (full): $$\ce{2MH <-->[{k3}][{k3r}] H2 + 2M}$$
